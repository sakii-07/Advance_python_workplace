## Project Title

Superstore Sales Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Superstore sales data to identify business trends, customer behavior, product performance, regional sales, and profitability using Python-based data analysis techniques.

## Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

## Tasks Performed

* Imported and explored the dataset.
* Cleaned the data by checking missing values and correcting data types.
* Converted date columns into proper datetime format.
* Performed descriptive statistics.
* Analyzed sales, profit, customers, products, categories, and regions.
* Created multiple visualizations including:

  * Monthly Sales Trend
  * Sales by Category
  * Sales by Region
  * Profit Analysis
  * Customer Analysis
  * Product Analysis
* Generated business insights and recommendations based on the analysis.

## Key Insights

* Identified the highest-selling products.
* Determined the most profitable categories and sub-categories.
* Analyzed regional and state-wise sales and profits.
* Found monthly sales trends over time.
* Identified top-performing customers based on sales.
* Compared sales and profit across different segments.

## Conclusion

The Exploratory Data Analysis helped uncover important business insights regarding sales performance, customer behavior, and profitability. These insights can support better business decisions, inventory management, marketing strategies, and revenue growth.
